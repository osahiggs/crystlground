About Us

Our Mission

Crystl Ground is an independent record label founded in 2022, helping artists and labels of all sizes move their music from idea to release without compromising identity.

As our label expands, we continue to build around an artist-first approach, supporting each stage of the release process with the tools, guidance, and systems needed to move your music forward properly.
